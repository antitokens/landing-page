QUANTUM TOKENOMICS

Stage 1

6 months

1.1 Re-brand
Initialisation of the Quantum-like Memetokenomics with setting up of Antitoken Webpage, Antitoken App, Antitoken Forum and Antitoken Team. Holders of ANTI and PRO will partake in simple governance decisions of the project and infrastructure for this will be set up in this step. More details in Whitepaper 1.0
1.2 Catalyst
Interaction Laws will be coded in a Catalyst program, and it will decide how ANTI and PRO interact on chain. Different proportions and magnitudes of ANTI and PRO will result in different interactions in the Catalyst program, with each interaction being unique and leading to unique consequences. More details in Whitepaper 1.0
1.3 Emissions
ANTI and PRO will interact in the Catalyst program upon deposit by users to emit new utility tokens. Newly emitted tokens will be fungible as well as non-fungible, determined by the Catalyst program. Emissions will follow rudimentary quantum-like principles. More details in Whitepaper 1.0
1.4 Radiation
ANTI and PRO interactions will contain a pseudo-random element in the Catalyst, which will radiate additional 'photonic radiation' in form of fungible micro-tokens; this Radiation is in addition to the Emissions in the previous step. Micro-token radiation will be indeterministic and probabilistic in nature. More details in Whitepaper 1.0
1.5 Entanglement
In this final step of Stage 1, ANTI, PRO, the Emissions and the Radiation will normalise to their entangled state determined by the market forces. The onchain constraints on ANTI, PRO, the Emissions and the Radiation will dynamically determine the entangled state of the new system. More details in Whitepaper 1.0
